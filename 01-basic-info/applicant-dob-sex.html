Why do we ask this?

We use your Social Security Number to check information about to find out what you qualify for as we go through these steps. With your Social Security Number, we may be able to sign you up for services today.

You do not have to provide your Social Security Number right now, but if you choose not to, you will be asked to provide documents that prove things like your income sources.
